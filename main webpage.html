About Us

Hindu Swayamsevak Sangh (HSS) Thailand is a voluntary, non-profit, cultural organization that aims to preserve and promote the Hindu way of life through various cultural, educational, and social activities. Inspired by the values of unity, discipline, and selfless service, HSS strives to build a vibrant Hindu community in Thailand.

We organize regular gatherings, youth training programs, cultural events, and community service initiatives that help individuals connect with their roots and contribute positively to society.
