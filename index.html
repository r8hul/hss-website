Who We Are

Rooted in Dharma,
Dedicated to Service

Hindu Swayamsevak Sangh (HSS) is a global Hindu cultural and service organisation active in over 50 countries. In Thailand, we have been nurturing a vibrant community of dedicated volunteers — swayamsevaks — since the early 2000s.

Through our weekly shakhas, seva projects, and cultural programmes, we strive to instil strong values, build character, and contribute positively to Thai society while preserving our rich Hindu heritage for generations to come.

Our Core Programmes

Every programme is designed to strengthen individuals and communities through values, service, and cultural pride.

🪔

Shakhas

Weekly gatherings combining physical exercises, surya namaskar, games, Sanskrit shlokas, and intellectual discussions. The shakha is the heartbeat of HSS — building discipline, friendship, and character.

Seva Projects

From food distribution and educational support to disaster relief, our volunteers serve the community year-round. Seva is not just an activity for us — it is a way of life rooted in dharma.

Festivals & Culture

Diwali, Holi, Raksha Bandhan, Navratri and more — our cultural events bring the Hindu community of Thailand together to celebrate our living heritage with warmth, music, and joy.
