Regular Programmes

Our Core Activities

🧘

Weekly Shakhas

Physical drills, surya namaskar, khel (games), and bauddhik (intellectual discussions) to build character and camaraderie.

👦

Bala Gokulam

Fun and enriching sessions for children — stories, arts, bhajans, and language to nurture young minds.

🎆

Festivals

Cultural performances, puja, and community bonding during Diwali, Holi, Raksha Bandhan, and other key festivals.

🏕️

Leadership Camps

Outdoor camps and workshops that build teamwork, resilience, and initiative in youth and young adults.

❤️

Seva Projects

From relief efforts to education drives — hands-on opportunities to serve the community with dedication.
