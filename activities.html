Our Programs & Activities

Weekly Shakhas

Structured gatherings including physical exercises, games, and discussions to build physical fitness, mental discipline, and community bonding.

Bala Gokulam

Programs designed for children to learn Indian culture, values, language, and arts through fun and engaging formats.

Festivals & Celebrations

Community-wide events to celebrate major Hindu festivals like Diwali, Holi, Raksha Bandhan, and more, with cultural performances and rituals.

Leadership Camps

Annual training camps and workshops to empower youth with leadership skills, teamwork, and dharmic guidance.

Seva Projects

Volunteer-driven service activities including food drives, educational support, and disaster relief work in local communities.
